About Afyalink

Connecting Communities to Quality Healthcare

Afyalink is a revolutionary healthcare facility locator platform designed to bridge the gap between Kenyan communities and healthcare services. Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone by providing accurate, up-to-date information about healthcare facilities across Kenya.

Our Mission

To empower Kenyan communities by providing easy access to comprehensive healthcare facility information, enabling informed healthcare decisions and improving overall public health outcomes.

Key Features

Interactive Map

Find healthcare facilities near you with our user-friendly interactive map interface.

Detailed Information

Access comprehensive details about facilities, including services offered and contact information.

Advanced Filtering

Filter facilities by type, services, and location to find exactly what you need.

Mobile Friendly

Access Afyalink on any device with our responsive, mobile-first design.

How It Works

Search

Use our interactive map or search filters to find healthcare facilities in your area.

Compare

View detailed information about multiple facilities to compare services and features.

Navigate

Get directions to your chosen facility using our integrated mapping system.
